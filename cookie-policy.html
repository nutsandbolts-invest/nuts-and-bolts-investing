Cookie Policy.

How cookies and similar technologies may be used on the Nuts & Bolts Investing website.

Last updated: 28 April 2026

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how they may be used on this website, and how you can control them. It should be read alongside our Privacy Policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They can help a website work properly, remember preferences, understand how people use the site, or support services provided by third-party tools.

Similar technologies, such as pixels, local storage, and tags, may also be used for similar purposes.

2. Types of Cookies

Cookies can generally be grouped into the following categories:

  • Strictly necessary cookies: needed for a website or service to work properly.
  • Functional cookies: used to remember choices or improve the way a site works.
  • Analytics cookies: used to understand how visitors use a website.
  • Marketing cookies: used to measure advertising or show relevant marketing.

3. How This Website Uses Cookies

Nuts & Bolts Investing is currently a simple static website. We do not currently intend to use advertising cookies, remarketing pixels, or advanced analytics tracking directly on the website.

However, some cookies or similar technologies may be set by services used to operate the website or provide features, including:

  • Vercel: hosting and security-related processing for the website.
  • Kit: embedded mailing list forms and email signup functionality.
  • FormSubmit: contact form delivery, where applicable.
  • Gumroad: course purchase pages or checkout links if you click through to buy a product.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Third-party services may set their own cookies when their forms, scripts, checkout pages, or other tools are used. We do not control all cookies set by third-party services.

For example, if you use a Kit signup form or click through to Gumroad, those services may process information under their own cookie and privacy policies.

5. Consent for Non-Essential Cookies

Under UK rules, non-essential cookies usually require consent. If we add analytics, advertising pixels, or other non-essential tracking tools in future, we will update this policy and, where required, add a cookie banner or consent mechanism.

Strictly necessary cookies, such as those needed for security or basic site functionality, may be used without consent where permitted by law.

6. Managing Cookies

You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers let you block cookies, delete existing cookies, or receive a warning before a cookie is stored.

If you block all cookies, some features may not work properly, including embedded signup forms, checkout pages, or other third-party services.

7. Browser Controls

The exact steps depend on your browser. You can usually find cookie controls in the privacy or security section of your browser settings. Common browser help pages include:

  • Google Chrome cookie settings
  • Safari privacy and website data settings
  • Firefox cookie and site data settings
  • Microsoft Edge cookies and site permissions

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time, especially if we add analytics, advertising, or other tools that use cookies. The latest version will always be available on this page, with the date at the top showing when it was last updated.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this Cookie Policy, contact us at support@nutsandboltsinvesting.com.

ISA basics

Stocks and Shares ISA explained.

A simple overview of what a Stocks and Shares ISA is and why UK beginners hear about it so often.

What is it?

A Stocks and Shares ISA is a UK account that can hold investments such as funds, ETFs, shares, and bonds, subject to ISA rules and annual limits.

Why beginners care

The main appeal is tax treatment. Returns inside an ISA are generally sheltered from UK income tax and capital gains tax, but rules can change.

What to check first

Before opening one, understand fees, investment choice, access, risk, time frame, and whether investing is suitable for money you may need soon.

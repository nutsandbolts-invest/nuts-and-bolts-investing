# Nuts & Bolts Investing Brand Brief

## Business

Nuts & Bolts Investing is an information-as-a-service business for absolute beginner investors.

The goal is to help complete beginners start investing simply and confidently.

## Core Website Goals

- Grow the email list
- Sell a paid beginner investing course
- Capture leads for future offers
- Build trust and credibility through educational content
- Support multiple revenue streams beyond one subscription product

## Potential Revenue Streams

- Paid beginner investing course
- Membership
- Mailing list
- Affiliate recommendations
- Templates
- Sponsorships
- Premium educational products

## Audience

Absolute beginner investors who want calm, practical guidance without hype.

## Tone

- Calm
- Practical
- Beginner-friendly
- Trustworthy
- Not hype-driven

## Brand Direction

- Deep navy background
- Mint/green accents
- Clean, modern, practical design
- Minimal but warm
- Avoid flashy trading aesthetics

## Positioning Notes

- Focus on clarity over complexity
- Make investing feel understandable and approachable
- Help beginners take simple first steps with confidence
- Avoid jargon-heavy language and aggressive sales tactics

Growth basics

Compound interest explained.

A plain-English guide to why growth on previous growth matters over long periods.

What does compounding mean?

Compounding means earning growth on earlier growth. Over long periods, this can make time an important part of investing.

Why it matters

Beginners often focus only on the starting amount. Compounding helps explain why patience, consistency, fees, and time frame can matter.

What to remember

Compounding is not guaranteed. Investments can fall as well as rise, and returns are not smooth or certain.

Why Nuts & Bolts exists

Most investing content starts halfway through the conversation.

Beginners are often told to compare platforms, choose funds, open accounts, and think long term before anyone has explained the basic parts clearly.

Nuts & Bolts Investing is built to slow that down. The goal is to explain the simple building blocks first, so you can make better sense of the decisions that come later.

Nutshell explanation

What is a share?

A share is a small piece of ownership in a company. You learn what that means before looking at charts or opinions.

Plain-English term

What is diversification?

Spreading money across different investments so one result does not carry everything.

Beginner mistake

Starting with products first

Many beginners compare products before understanding accounts, risk, time frame, and fees.
